Style Guidelines

For continuity, the same color-scheme will be used on each of the pages. Every page will have clearly defined navigation in the header and contact information in the header and contact/social media information in the footer. It is important that the webpages be aesthetically appealing in a style that could be labeled as "essentialism." The tone of the writing and the content will convey authenticity and demonstrate creativity and multifaceted interests, experience, and expertise.
